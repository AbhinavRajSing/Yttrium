Big Internet companies are making suboptimal decisions to push market valuation. We see more and more ads in search results and not enough care for the people who are actually creating content. Ahrefs intends to stay independent and focus on helping people to make useful content and to make it more accessible with an ultimate goal to make a search engine sharing profit with content creators. The tools we provide will help you to improve your website, to find a topic to write about or to track your website's performance. We will guide you on the way with learning materials on our blog and Youtube channel. While all our learning materials and some of the tools will stay free - consider supporting us by subscribing to Ahrefs.
